In late 2019, Andrew Lake suffered from a seizure in his sleep, which thankfully for him (albeit traumatic for all involved), his girlfriend, Siobhan, was present and able to call 911 immediately. Lake was rushed to Beth Israel Hospital in Boston, where they treated him for a dislocated shoulder (due to the intensity of the seizure) and began running CAT Scans and MRIs. Little did we know, that his seizure was just the catalyst towards months of more testing, unclear diagnosis, surgeries, medication, radiation, chemotherapy, and physical therapy.

Further testing found that Lake had a tumor in his brain, which would need to be operated on immediately. After the invasive and painful surgery to remove the tumor, Andrew had just begun the path to recovery. The surgery wasn’t able to remove the entirety of the tumor but with the help of radiation and chemotherapy, the doctors are hopeful that this will improve his current state.

Andrew Lake, a son to Jim and Lynn, brother to Jocelyn, uncle to Lillian, and partner to Siobhan McHugh-Mullane, and together they have a dog named, Olive.

Andrew is someone who has never hesitated to offer a helping hand whether it’s volunteering to help a friend move or providing a listening ear to those who need it. Lake also is a Marine, serving two tours in Iraq during his service. Each day, Andrew has found countless ways to serve his community of Plymouth, his country, and his loved ones. Andrew may not ask for help, as he is an independent soul but it’s time that join together and support someone who has risked his life for others. The road to recovery will be physically and emotionally painful, and although we cannot carry the burden of that, we can help with the financial burden of the astronomical health care costs which are now piling up.

So we will ask - on behalf of this beloved Marine, brother, partner, son, friend, and musician, we ask to give as much as you can.

What's Happening?

LakeFest! We're having a benefit to help raise money for Andrew's journey. All money raised for this event will go straight to Andrew, so please be as generous as you possibly can.

Here are a few things going on at the event

Brunch

Brunch will be provided by The New World Tavern. Cost is included in your ticket price! So come hungry.

Raffles

There will be raffle tickets available for purchase. Prizes range from gift baskets to event tickets. There will also be a 50-50 raffle in which half the proceeds will go to the winner and the other half will go to Andrew.

Live Music

There will be live music throughout the event performed by The Barnacles They are a Classic Rock Cover band, so bring your dancing shoes.

Full Bar

The New World Tavern offers a full bar, so there will be alcohol available for purchase.
